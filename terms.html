Terms of Service

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Bunofeed. These Terms of Service outline the rules, obligations, and refund policies governing the use of the Bunofeed website at bunofeed.in. By placing an order, you agree to these conditions in full.

1. Placing Orders

Orders can be submitted directly through our digital store platform. All orders are subject to availability and acceptance:

  • Pincode Verification: Deliveries are strictly limited to listed serviceable pincodes. If your pincode is outside our active delivery circle, the order form will notify and prevent checkout.
  • Product Weights: Products have variants (sizes like 400gm, 250gm, 1.25kg, etc.). Selling price and crossed-out MRP are specific to each product weight variant. Please verify your selection in the product modal before completing payment.

2. Payments & Settlement

Payment on Bunofeed is settled securely in Indian Rupees (INR) at checkout using our Razorpay integration. We support Debit Cards, Credit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Net Banking. Order processing begins once Razorpay confirms transaction SUCCESS.

3. Shipping & Delivery

We aim to pack and dispatch orders within 24 hours of successful placement.

  1. Transit Timeline: Average delivery takes 3 to 7 business days, depending on location and courier accessibility.
  2. Delivery Rates: Standard shipping charges are calculated at checkout. Orders exceeding the specific limit set in the admin dashboard (e.g., ₹499) qualify for Free Shipping.
Statutory Declaration on Delivery: Ownership transfers to the customer once the courier delivers the package to the address provided during checkout. Correct delivery address details remain the customer's responsibility.

4. Cancellations, Returns & Refund Policy

We want you to love your wholesome health food. If something is not right, here is how we handle returns and refunds:

  • Cancellations: You can request a cancellation of your order through our tracking page (My Orders) at any time before dispatch. If confirmed, a full refund is settled instantly.
  • Damaged or Wrong Products: If you receive a physically damaged package, defective items, or incorrect product variants, you are entitled to a full replacement or refund. Submit a "Request Return" from our tracking screen within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Evidence uploads: When requesting a return, please select the appropriate reason and upload a clear photo/jpeg of the parcel condition or item error.
  • Refund Settlement: Approved refunds are credited automatically back to the original payment source (UPI/Bank/Card) processed via Razorpay. This typically reflects in your bank statement within 5–7 business days as per banking norms.

5. Modification of Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms, prices, shipping thresholds, and promotional offers at any time to reflect business and freight adjustments. These updates instantly sync to the website on save.
