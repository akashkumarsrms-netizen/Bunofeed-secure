Understanding Your Protein Needs

Protein is essential for building and repairing tissues, producing enzymes, and supporting immune function. The right amount depends on your body weight, activity level, age, and goals. Active individuals and those building muscle typically need more protein than sedentary adults. For most healthy adults, 1.2–2.2g of protein per kg of body weight per day is a good range.
