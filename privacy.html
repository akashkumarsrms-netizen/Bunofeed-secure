Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Bunofeed, available from bunofeed.in, we prioritize the privacy and security of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy documents the types of information we collect, record, and how we handle it securely in compliance with standard regulations.

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase products, track orders, or contact us through our website, we may collect the following personal identifier data:

  • Identity Details: Your full name, delivery address, and contact details.
  • Contact Details: Your email address, billing/delivery phone numbers.
  • Order Details: Purchased product names, item quantities, total cost, and special delivery instructions.
  • Payment Ref Details: Transaction IDs, receipt IDs, and transaction status generated via Razorpay. We do NOT collect or store your financial passwords or full credit/debit numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly utilized for core operational and transactional pathways:

  1. To pack, fulfill, ship, and deliver your ordered items from our warehouse.
  2. To process transactions securely via our integrated payment gateway (Razorpay).
  3. To handle cancellation and return workflows seamlessly, verifying genuine grievances.
  4. To contact you for address verification or dispatch tracking updates.
  5. To send promotional newsletters or alerts ONLY if you opted-in.
Important Security Protocol: We do not sell, leak, rent, or trade your personal information with third-party advertisers or brokers. Your data is handled strictly to service your order.

3. Cookies and Log Files

Bunofeed utilizes standard client-side state mechanisms (cookies and localStorage) to maintain your shopping cart experience. For instance, when you add a specific quantity of Peanut Butter or Oats to your cart, these selections persist inside your browser cache so that you don't lose items during navigation.

4. Data Security

Our infrastructure coordinates all requests over secure, encrypted SSL channels. Payment processing is managed on Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure to guarantee the highest safety limits against fraud and unauthorized credential access.

5. Questions or Opt-Out

If you have questions about this policy, or would like us to remove your past contact/order registry logs from our system, please connect with us:
