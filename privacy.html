At Bunofeed, we believe in radical transparency. We do not sell your personal data. We only collect the bare minimum information needed to process your orders, process payments, and dispatch healthy nutrition directly to your doorstep.

1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Bunofeed ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, processes, and protects your information when you visit our website at bunofeed.in and place orders for our peanut butter, rolled oats, and muesli products. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when placing an order, subscribing to banners/newsletters, or contacting our support team.

Identity Info Name, Phone number, Email ID
Shipping Coordinates Full Address, Pincode
Transactions Payment status, Order ID

Note: We do NOT store your credit card or debit card numbers on our servers. All transaction operations are funneled through RBI-regulated, SSL-encrypted modern payment gateways like Razorpay.

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is parsed with strict adherence to data minimization standards. Core use-cases include:

  • Processing and dispatching your physical orders.
  • Sharing order confirmations and delivery status updates via email/phone.
  • Authorizing transaction queries on Razorpay.
  • Improving our website’s responsive layouts and product catalog.

4 Third-Party Sharing

We do not lease, sell, or rent your personal data to advertisers. We only share critical pieces of information with verified partners who make delivery possible:

  • Logistics Partners: Delivery services (Blue Dart, Delhivery) to ship your packages.
  • Payment Gateway Providers: Razorpay to process payments safely.
  • Legal Compliance: Government authorities, if required by Indian law.

5 Cookies and Tracking

We use standard local storage configurations and cookies inside your web browser to optimize delivery speeds:

  • Retaining your order log values in the local state.
  • Preserving responsive user theme preferences.

You can choose to disable cookies easily in your browser settings, though doing so might disrupt your checkout experience.

6 Data Security

We place high emphasis on server health and system integrity. We utilize SSL encryption for all data transport protocols. Additionally, we enforce strong credential hashing rules inside our store settings.

7 User Rights

You have full ownership of your personal data. That includes rights to:

  • View copy of all information stored under your phone number.
  • Request corrections or updates.
  • Request complete deletion of your order history logs.
